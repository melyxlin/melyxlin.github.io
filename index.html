Hi! I'm Melissa! I am a junior majoring in CS at Boston University.

This summer, I will be interning at Google as STEP intern. Previously, I interned at Fidelity Investments as a Software Engineer Intern where I developed an web application for the QA department and was a technical intern at Open Vision Technology doing work with semiconductor handling aligners.

During the school year, I am a course assistant for BU' Computer Science Department, where I get to share my passion and enthusiasm for computing with fellow students. I also play an activate roles in organizing several hackathons around Boston, one being BostonHacks, BU'premier hackathon and another being TechTogether Boston, the largest female, femme, and nonbinary hackathon in Boston.

When I am not programming, you can find me cooking, eating noodles, reading, cycling, crocheting, or practicing the piano.
