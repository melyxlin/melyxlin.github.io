Hello World!

Hi! I'm Melissa! I am second year CS major at Boston University.

This past summer, I interned at Fidelity Investments as a Software Engineer Intern. Previously, I was a technical intern at Open Vision Technology doing work with semiconductor handling aligners.

During the school year, I am a course assistant for BU's Computer Science Department, where I get to share my passion and enthusiasm for computing with fellow students. I am also one of the Project Managers for Hack4Impact, an organization that helps create software for non-profits. Finally, I play an active roles in organizing a few hackathons, being the Head of Marketing for BostonHacks, BU's premier hackathon and the Email Outreach Lead for TechTogether Boston, the largest female and nonbinary hackathons in the world.

When I am not programming, you can find me cooking, eating noodles, reading, cycling, crocheting, or practicing the piano.
